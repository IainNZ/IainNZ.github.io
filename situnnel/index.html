Staten Island Subway Tunnels

Maps and documents regarding the many proposals to build mass-transit tunnels to connect the New York City borough of Staten Island (Richmond Country) with the boroughs of Brooklyn and Manhattan.

A note on style: This page is intended to be both informational and a "literature review." Every piece of information should be linked back to a primary source as much as possible. In descending order of preference: government records (★★★), newspapers of note (★★), "primary source" personal websites (★), and finally Wikipedia.
