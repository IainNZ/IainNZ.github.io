Locations from "City of Sedition"

"City of Sedition" by John Strausbaugh is a book about New York before, during, and after the American Civil War. Apart from being a great book in general, one thing I found myself noticing during reading it is all the addresses it mentions. The majority are in downtown Manhattan, a reflection of where the vast majority of the population lived at the time. As a modern-day resident of the area, who recognized many of the addresses but not all, I wanted to map out all the addresses in the book that I could and see what those locations look like today.
